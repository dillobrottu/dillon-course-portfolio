Rockshow

Concert Film | 1980 | Wings

Rockshow is a 1980 concert film of the North American tour of Paul McCartney and Wings. The film features live performances and songs that can be found on the Wings over America album and the Wings Over the World documentary. Rockshow is my favorite concert film as it shows the talent of McCartney and includes my favorite versions of each song performed. I always listen to the Rockshow version of each song as it to me sounds better and more raw compared to the studio album versions. Rockshow is in my opinion one of the best concert films.

Pink Floyd: Live at Pompeii

Concert Film | 1972 | Pink Floyd

Pink Floyd: Live at Pompeii is a 1972 concert film of the performance by Pink Floyd at the Pompeii amphitheatre. Pink Floyd's performance is a strange story, as it is a concert with out an audience. Pink Floyd performance at the ancient roman amphitheatre is an experience for any person who watches and listens. Each composition is remade to fit only 4 people to perform which creates a more raw experience. Live at Pompeii is an experience and shows the talent of the Pink Floyd.

Exit... Stage Left

Concert Film | 1982 | Rush

Exit... Stage Left is a 1982 concert film of the performance by Rush at the Montreal Forum. Exit... Stage Left features music from the album of the same name, but features some exclusive versions of some songs. The versions of each song are greater in quality to the studio versions in my opinion. The energy of each perfomance gives a feeling that can't be replicated. Exit... Stage Left is a great concert film and shows some of the best music performed by Rush.

The Who at Kilburn: 1977

Concert | 1977 | The Who

The Who at Kilburn is a 1977 concert film of the two live performances by The Who at the Gaumont State Cinema and London Coliseum. The Who are one of the most energitic rock bands in history, and the Kilburn performances captures the energy of that era. The performances of each song are even more energitic and raw compared to the studio versions. The film shows each member of The Who at their peak music and instrument performance. the Who at Kilburn is a moment that captures the power that The Who on film.

The Song Remains the Same

Concert Film | 1976 | Led Zeppelin

The Song Remains the Same is a 1976 concert film of three nights of concerts at the Madison Square garden performed by Led Zeppelin. The film features the live perfomance of Led Zeppelin and the music of the album of the same name. The performances of each song capture the members of Led Zeppelin at their peak performance. Each version of the songs performed capture the energy and raw power of Led Zeppelin. The Song Remains the Same shows the talent and power that Led Zeppelin had at their prime.

The Bruce McMouse Show

Concert Film | 1972 | Wings

The Bruce McMouse Show is a 1972 concert film from Paul McCartney and Wing's European tour. The film features an animated story of how the band came to meet the inimitable impresario Bruce McMouse. The previously never-before-seen film shows excluse versions of music performed by Wings. Each version performed features a more raw and energetic version compared to the studio version counterparts. The BruceMcMouse Show is a fun experience that captures that era of time in a moment.
