The Living Web

At the start of the course, the course portfolio felt like just another assignment.
But as I completed more lessons, it felt more personal. Like the essay said, the more I added to my personal website, the more personal it became.
Being able to revisit previous lessons on my own page, rather than a file explorer feels more personal.
Like putting books on a shelf instead of stacking them in the closet, having a dedicated site for myself feels more personal and individualistic.

Part 2

The purpose of the course portfolio is to give an insight to the owner of the site.
Allowing a user to see into the experiences that I have taken into following lessons of the course.
It doesn't just allow a user to look into my experiences, but also myself.
It allows me to look back into the lessons I have taken and learned and allow me to revisit them whenever I need to.
The purpose of the Professional Portfolio website will allow another user, or myself, to experience or re-experience the projects and courses I have completed.
