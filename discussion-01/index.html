Part 2

In the modern era, sites that allow for the creation of easy made media have become a dominant part of internet culture.
Though tools like social media, AI, and video sites are useful, creating a personal website for yourself can be better than the alternatives.
Making your own personal site allows for full customization and personalization of your page compared to the alternatives.
Other sites have a pre-made template for you to work in, while making your own site allows you to tailor make and personalize what ever you want for your site.
Making a personal site is also more professional and allows you to show your skills as a web developer to anyone that visits it.

The benefits of learning a markup language like HTML and CSS is that it allows you to understand how your site works.
Knowing what goes on underneath the hood of your site can allow you to make alterations and fixes to it fast and easy.
Compared to using a template site or AI, using HTML allows you to work more personally with the site and allow you to work more in-depth compared
to the tools that the template sites can give you. And if something were to go wrong in the site, knowing HTML can allow you to fix it yourself instead of trying to rely on external methods.
